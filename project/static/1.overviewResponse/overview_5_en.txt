Self-efficacy refers to one’s belief in their capacity to do well and succeed in specific situations. In the context of studying, it refers to the student’s trust in themselves as a learner. It relates to the student’s confidence in themselves and their skills. 

Self-efficacy is shown in the choices of activities the student engages in. The higher one’s self-efficacy is, the better one can handle stressful situations. Self-efficacy also correlates with academic achievement.
