A learning environment is a place where the students feel encouraged to study and their environment inspires them. They feel supported and there is space and encouragement for asking questions. In an ideal learning environment, the students have the freedom to express themselves and the methods to learn and teach are diverse. In such a space, the teachers should understand individuals’ needs and guide them accordingly. 

In general, there are various things affecting the learning process and they consist of multiple environmental factors; where students come from, their friends and family, peers, as well as the actual physical space are all things that can easily affect one’s motivation to study and learn. These factors are good to keep in mind when thinking about your result, as these help you understand what affects your learning process and you get a better understanding of what you need to be able to focus and learn more effectively.
